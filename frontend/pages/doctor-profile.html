About Dr. Sarah Johnson

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a board-certified cardiologist with over 12 years of experience in cardiovascular medicine. She specializes in preventive cardiology, heart disease management, and cardiac imaging. Dr. Johnson completed her medical degree at Harvard Medical School and her cardiology fellowship at Johns Hopkins Hospital.
